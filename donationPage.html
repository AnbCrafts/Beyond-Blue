Make a donation

Beyond Blue is dedicated to improving the lives of individuals, families and communities affected by anxiety, depression and suicide – and creating change to protect everyone’s mental health.

Your donation will help Beyond Blue to:

  • reduce the impact of depression, anxiety and suicide by supporting people to protect their mental health and to recover when they are unwell

  • reduce people’s experiences of stigma and discrimination

  • improve people’s opportunities to get effective support and services at the right time.

We are incredibly grateful to the many individuals, families, communities and organisations across Australia who generously support our work.
